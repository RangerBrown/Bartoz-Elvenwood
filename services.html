Before You Decide ಕೇಳ್ಬೇಕಾದ ಪ್ರಶ್ನೆಗಳು

Questions homeowners ask before starting. We'd rather answer them upfront.

Will the price change after I pay the booking amount?

No. Your approved quote is your final price. We don't escalate costs during design or production. The scope, materials, and price are locked before any payment.

What if I want to cancel after booking?

Clear, written cancellation terms before any payment. If production hasn't started, you can cancel with a full refund. No 'non-refundable booking fee' traps.

Will anyone respond to me after I pay?

Your designer's direct WhatsApp number from day one. Project updates at every stage. We reply within 4 hours on working days. You'll never chase us.

How do I know the finished work will match the design?

Our Biesse CNC machines cut to 0.1mm precision. Every panel matches the technical drawing exactly. Visit the factory and see for yourself.

Will you use the materials you promised?

Visit the Experience Centre and touch every material we'll use. Century Ply, Greenlam, Hettich — same brands, same grade. No substitutions.

Will my kitchen handle Bangalore's humidity?

BWP (Boiling Water Proof) plywood and moisture-resistant laminates come standard. Not upsells. Your kitchen is built for monsoons, not just photos.
