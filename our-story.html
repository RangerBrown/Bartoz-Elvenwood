OUR STORY

Six of us had worked in this industry long enough to know what homeowners go through. So we built Elvenwood.

Delivering what we promise is the whole point.

The Problem

Missed timelines. Vague pricing. Work that doesn't match the design. The carpenter who vanished mid-project. The "premium materials" that turned out to be something else entirely.

In Bangalore's interior design industry, broken promises aren't the exception — they're the norm. And homeowners have learned to expect disappointment.

Our Solution

Six of us had worked in this industry long enough to know what homeowners go through. We pooled our experience across sales, design, engineering, production, and operations — and built Elvenwood. Dilip joined us to lead execution on the ground, because delivering what we promise is the whole point.

One designer handles your project from start to finish. Everything is built in our own 12,000 sq ft factory. Italian Biesse CNC machines cut every panel to 0.1mm precision. No outsourcing, no subcontractors.

The price we quote is the price you pay. The date we commit to is the date we finish. The 3D render you approve is exactly what appears in your home.
