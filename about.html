About

At Elvenwood, we are a dedicated team of designers and engineers building premium home interiors in Bangalore. We don't outsource—everything is crafted in our own 12,000 sq ft factory with uncompromising precision.

Every home has
a heartbeat.

We've all heard the stories.
The carpenter who vanished mid-project.
The timeline that stretched from weeks to months.
The final bill that looked nothing like the quote.

In Bangalore's interior industry, broken promises
are the norm. We decided to do things differently.

One designer handles your project from start to finish.
Everything is built in our own 12,000 sq ft factory.

Team

  • Client

    Your vision is our
    driving force.

    Your lifestyle, preferences, and even your unarticulated needs are the core of our design process. Share your thoughts openly. Even if they don't have a clear shape yet, your ideas form the starting point for our entire team.

  • Project Management

    Understanding you,
    setting the direction.

    Our sales and management team listens to how you live. We ask the right questions and give you an honest, upfront sense of budget and timeline. We ensure we deeply understand your goals before a single sketch is drawn.

  • Design Management

    Translating ideas
    into beautiful realities.

    Your dedicated designer is with you from the first sketch to the final 3D render. They translate your vision into a concrete plan, ensuring nothing moves to production without your enthusiastic approval of the design and materials.

  • Production Management

    Precision engineering
    in our own facility.

    Our production heads oversee the manufacturing of your furniture in our 12,000 sq ft factory. Every panel is cut on Italian CNC machines with zero compromises on quality. You are always welcome to visit the factory and watch your home being built.

  • Construction Management

    Bringing it all together
    in your space.

    Our in-house installation leads ensure the design intent is perfectly executed on-site. They stay until the very end, managing the assembly logic and walking you through the finished home until you are completely satisfied.
